Unordered list

Such a list can represent items where the order is not important, e.g. a list of ingredients required for preparing a dish or a drink.

Ingredients required for making a coffee:

  • 15g coffee ground
  • a cup
  • water

Ordered list

The ordered list can be used if the order of the items matters, e.g. when describing an instruction. The order of steps that must be done is important.

How to prepare the coffee?

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Put 15g of the coffee ground into the cup
  3. Boil 200ml of water
  4. Pour water into a cup
  5. Optional: add milk as you wish
