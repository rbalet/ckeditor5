Let's use the coffee recipe from the demo in section Ordered and unordered lists and use the list styles feature.

Ingredients required for making a coffee:

  • 15g coffee ground
  • a cup
  • water

How to prepare the coffee?

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Put 15g of the coffee ground into the cup
  3. Boil 200ml of water
  4. Pour water into a cup
  5. Optional: add milk as you wish
